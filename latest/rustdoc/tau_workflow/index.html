Linear pipeline runner for tau agentic workflows.

See docs/superpowers/specs/2026-05-12-tau-workflow-design.md for the design + format. v1 supports linear sequential workflows defined under workflows/*.toml, with step kinds agent.run and tool.call. Append-only JSONL persistence under <scope>/.tau/workflow-runs/ enables --resume with strict drift checking.
